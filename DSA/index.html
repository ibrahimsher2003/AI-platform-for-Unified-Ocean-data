Platform Overview

India, with its vast coastline and rich marine biodiversity, relies heavily on the health of its ocean ecosystems. CMLRE plays a pivotal role in collecting, managing, and analysing a broad spectrum of marine data.

This intelligent digital platform integrates heterogeneous datasets from oceanography, taxonomy, morphology, and molecular biology into a single unified system, powered by AI.
